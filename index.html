MOTTO OF OUR CLUB  

At our organization, we are dedicated to serving the Biotechnology Industry Organization (BIT) by providing a range of valuable resources and opportunities. Our primary aim is to facilitate the development of the knowledge, skills, networks, and experiences that are essential for preparing our members for successful biotechnology-related careers. We recognize that the biotechnology industry is constantly evolving and that staying up-to-date with the latest trends, technologies, and best practices is critical for success. That's why we offer a range of educational programs, training sessions, and networking events designed to help our members stay ahead of the curve. Our team is made up of experienced professionals who are passionate about the biotechnology industry and who are committed to supporting our members in achieving their career goals. Whether you're just starting out in your career or are a seasoned professional looking to take your skills to the next level, we're here to help.

HISTORY OF OUR CLUB  

The Biotechnology Club (BIOTS) of Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, was established in 2017 by a group of enthusiastic students who were passionate about biotechnology and its potential to solve real-world problems. The club was founded with the aim of providing a platform for students to learn about the latest advances in biotechnology, conduct research, and develop innovative solutions to challenges in healthcare, agriculture, and environmental protection.The BIOTS club has made a significant contribution to the technical and entrepreneurial culture at BIT Mesra. It has helped to produce some of the most talented engineers and entrepreneurs in India.
